      Good Exam Answer

      21st-century teaching methodologies are learner-centered approaches that promote active participation, critical thinking, collaboration, creativity, and the effective use of ICT. Examples include Inquiry-Based Learning, Problem-Based Learning, Project-Based Learning, Collaborative Learning, Flipped Classroom, Blended Learning, Differentiated Instruction, STEM/STEAM, ICT-Integrated Teaching, and the 5Es Model (Engage, Explore, Explain, Elaborate, Evaluate). These methodologies help learners develop competencies needed for the modern world and support the implementation of the Competence-Based Curriculum (CBC).
